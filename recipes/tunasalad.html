Random Tuna Salad

Random Tuna Salad isn't your Mom's Tuna Salad

How to Make Random Tuna Salad

If you're like me and not a fan of the relish and boiled eggs typically found in tuna salad, then you'll want to try this variation with apples and golden raisins. Serve on crackers, toasted whole grain bread, or wrapped in lettuce leaves.

Ingredients

  • 5 ounce can solid white tuna packed in water, drained
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons mayonnaise
  • 2 teaspoons brown mustard
  • 2 tablespoons red wine vinegar, or to taste
  • 1 medium apple (Cameo, Braeburn, Gala, or Fuji) -- peeled, cored, and chopped
  • 1 tablespoon golden raisins
  • 1/4 teaspoon cracked rainbow peppercorns
  • 1/4 teaspoon Greek seasoning

Instructions

  1. Mash tuna toghether with mayonnaise, brown mustard, red wine vinegar, pepper and Greek seasoing.
  2. Fold in apple and raisins and serve on crackers, toasted whole grain bread, or wrapped in lettuce leaves.
