Baked Apricot Chicken

Quick, easy, and tastes so good. The sauce is so good drizzled over the white rice.

How to Make Baked Apricot Chicken

Simply delicious. Quick and easy recipe for the busy homemaker. Serve with rice if desired.

Ingredients

  • 12 chicken thighs
  • 1 cup apricot preserves
  • 1 cup French dressing
  • 1 ounce package dry onion soup mix

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F
  2. In a medium bowl combine the jam, dressing, and soup mix.
  3. Place chicken pieces in a 9x13 inch baking dish. Pour apricot mixture over chicken and bake uncovered in the pre-heated oven for 50 to 60 minutes.
