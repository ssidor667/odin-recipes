Crazy Cake Recipe

Make this delicious Chocolate Crazy Cake and impress anyone.

How to Make Crazy Cake

This is literally the easiest chocolate cake that you are ever going to make. Another point is that this recipe doesn't use any perishable ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 cups white sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 3/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 1/2 cups water
  • 3/4 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons white vinegar

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350Â°F.
  2. Whisk together the dry ingredients in a large mixing bowl.
  3. Add in the wet ingredients then stir until smooth.
  4. Pour into an ungreased 9x13 pan.
  5. Bake in the oven for 20-25 minutes or until a cake tester inserted comes out clean.
  6. Cool in the pan and serve from the pan.
